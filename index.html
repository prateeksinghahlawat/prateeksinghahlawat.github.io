About Us

01

We’re a group of Individuals who are doing custom gifts for more than two years now. We’re finally coming together as a brand that aims to build a beautiful connection between you and your loved ones with the custom-made gift packages especially tailored for that special person in your life. How does it work?

02

After you fill the form given below, we carefully analyze all the details that you gave us about the recipient, and make a custom gift package especially railed for your loved one with a lot of useful and custom-made products. We then mail you the package details with all the items, further you can add, delete or change any item according to your wish. After finalizing the products, we pack them and hand deliver to you or your loved ones.
